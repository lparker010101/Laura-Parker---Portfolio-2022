About me

Hello!


I am Laura, a software developer passionate about using technology. I develop web applications using C#, HTML, CSS, SQL, ASP.NET, and Git.

My journey in becoming a developer goes back to when I taught as an English as a Second Language (ESL) teacher. I eagerly sought out technologies to maximize learning outcomes, increase engagement, and provide meaningful learning opportunities. This sparked a desire in me to learn software development and to transition into a technology role.

I graduated from Indiana University-Purdue University of Indianapolis with a Bachelor of Science degree in Elementary Education (K-6) with a minor in English as a Second Language (K-12). I recently graduated from the Software Development immersive learning program at Eleven Fifty Academy. In the years as teacher and student, I have put into practice excellent communication, time management, organization, training and education, and technical skills.

I look forward to connecting with you!
